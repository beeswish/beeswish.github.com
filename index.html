Applications

Rule of Three

Language: US-FR

Description

You get 2 breads for 3$, how much for 5 breads ?
Rule Of Three computes this with famous Rule Of Three aka Cross Product. Nothing more, nothing less!
The cool feature here is that you can gesture the cross-product on your mobile screen and immediately get the result!

Purpose

A very simple application to evaluate different frameworks and gesture features.
